Our Services

We supply high-grade HDPE materials and galvanized steel roofing products to global distributors and manufacturers, providing full sourcing, inspection and logistics support.

HDPE Bulk Supply

Wholesale High Density Poly Ethylene (HDPE) for packaging, molding, piping and industrial production lines. Reliable volumes and consistent grade options.

Galvanized Steel Roofing

International Logistics
